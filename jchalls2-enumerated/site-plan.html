Home page

Hello! My name is Curtis Halls, and I would like to extend a warm welcome to you as you explore my personal site project for the summer semester at BYU Idaho. The primary objective of this website is to provide a platform for me to showcase a carefully curated selection of the creative endeavors that have shaped my life. Throughout my journey, I have developed a profound passion for various artistic pursuits. One of my significant interests lies in amateur roleplaying game design. This captivating endeavor has allowed me to dedicate countless hours to the creation of immersive gaming experiences. I thoroughly enjoy the process of conceptualizing intricate storylines, designing unique gameplay mechanics, and crafting interactive worlds that captivate players' imaginations. While I acknowledge that my expertise in this field is still in its nascent stages, my unwavering enthusiasm and dedication drive me to continually improve and create captivating and memorable gaming experiences for players to enjoy. With each project I undertake, I eagerly embrace the opportunity to refine my skills and explore new horizons within the realm of game design. In addition to my foray into game design, I have also ventured into the realm of digital art. While I modestly refer to myself as an "extremely amateur" digital artist, I find immense joy and fulfillment in expressing my creativity through visual mediums. With each moment I have spent on my digital art, I embark on a journey of exploration and self-expression. Whether I am experimenting with different techniques of production or bringing to life captivating illustrations inspired by the vast natural world, I am continuously driven to push the boundaries of my artistic abilities. While the path of a digital artist can be challenging and ever-evolving, I relish the opportunity to learn, grow, and refine my skills with each new project I undertake. Beyond the realms of game design and digital art, another passion that has captivated me throughout my creative journey is fantasy writing. As a hobbyist fantasy writer, I have found solace in the realms of my own imagination, weaving intricate tales filled with vibrant characters, epic adventures, and awe-inspiring worlds. The act of crafting narratives that transport readers to enchanting realms has become a cherished part of my life. Each story I write allows me to immerse myself in the endless possibilities of the fantasy genre and engage in a dance with the muses of creativity. Whether it's a quest to save a kingdom or an intimate tale of personal growth, I find tremendous satisfaction in exploring the depths of my imagination through the written word. Additionally, I am humbled and proud to share that my love for poetry has led to the publication of my work. Poetry, with its ability to distill complex emotions into concise and poignant verses, has become an intimate form of expression for me. Through carefully chosen words and evocative imagery, I seek to evoke powerful emotions and create connections with readers. The act of crafting poetry allows me to explore the nuances of human experience, drawing inspiration from both the world around me and the depths of my own soul. With each poem I have penned, I endeavored to create a shared space where readers can embark on an emotional journey, finding solace, inspiration, and resonance in the power of language and the beauty of poetic expression. While my creative pursuits have been a significant part of my life, I have also nurtured academic interests that have shaped my aspirations for the future. Although my current focus lies in web design, my fascination with the field of Physics has been a constant companion. The profound mysteries of the universe and the fundamental laws that govern its workings have always intrigued me. While I am excited to pursue web design in my academic journey, my long-term goal is to merge my passion for scientific inquiry with my creative spirit, exploring the intersections of art, technology, and scientific discovery. Through this personal website, I aspire to bring together the various facets of my life and offer you a window into the diverse range of my artistic and academic pursuits. It is my sincere hope that as you explore this digital space, you will find inspiration, delight, and a shared sense of wonder. Thank you for taking the time to get to know me and my passions. Your visit is deeply appreciated, and I invite you to embark on this creative journey with me.
